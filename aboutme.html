Hi, I'm Alan!

I'm an engineer, a developer and just a nice guy :-)

I came to the United States from Almaty, Kazakhstan, and I quickly became attracted to the hands-on nature of engineering.

When I began my studies at UC San Diego, I found a passion in programmable circuits, such as those ran by Arduino and Raspberry Pi. In the blink of an eye, I became captivated by the methodology of artificial intelligence, which kick-started my specialization in Machine Learning and Controls during my undergraduate studies.

I recently graduated from UC San Diego with a Master of Science in Intelligent Systems, Robotics and Control.
